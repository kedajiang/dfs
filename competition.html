OVERVIEW

Design for Sitting Committee is calling for submission for its 7th competition. The competition is seeking for innovation thinking about the relationship between human and chairs. One Gold Medal of ¥20,000, two Silver Medals of ¥10,000, and four Bronze Medal of ¥5,000 are awarded for distinguished designs.


ELIGIBILITY

The deadline for submission is 2017.10.30
The submission requires a rendering picture (hand drawn or digital), a 3-view picture, and a brief of concept (2 pages maximum).
The project must be original.
One individual is limited to submit one project.
