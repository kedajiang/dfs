Design For Sitting

a competition for chair design

“Design For Sitting” competition has been held 6 times since 2002. This proposition came from the action “sit” triggering the thinking of the way of sitting, the condition of sitting, the mood of sitting, the aesthetic of sitting, and the culture of sitting. Briefly, this competition is aiming to represent the exploration of the idea of sitting from designers.
